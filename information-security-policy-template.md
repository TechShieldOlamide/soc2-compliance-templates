## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of [Organisation Name]'s information assets. It applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to company systems and data, and supports our SOC 2 compliance program.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all information systems, networks, applications, and data owned or managed by [Organisation Name], including cloud infrastructure, customer data, and internal business systems.

---

## 3. Information Security Objectives

- Protect customer and company data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction
- Ensure systems remain available to support business operations
- Maintain compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations including SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria
- Build and maintain customer trust through demonstrable security practices

---

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Leadership | Approves security policy and allocates resources for the security program |
| Information Security Lead | Owns the security program, policy maintenance, and audit coordination |
| Engineering Team | Implements technical security controls and maintains secure infrastructure |
| All Employees | Comply with security policies and report security concerns promptly |

---

## 5. Access Control

- Access to systems and data is granted based on the principle of least privilege
- Multi-factor authentication is required for all systems containing sensitive data
- Access is reviewed quarterly and removed immediately upon employee termination
- Privileged access is restricted to authorised personnel and logged

---

## 6. Data Classification and Handling

| Classification | Description | Handling Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Information intended for public disclosure | No special handling required |
| Internal | Information for internal use only | Access restricted to employees |
| Confidential | Sensitive business information | Access restricted, encryption required |
| Restricted | Highly sensitive data including customer PII | Strict access controls, encryption, audit logging |

---

## 7. Encryption

- All data classified as Confidential or Restricted is encrypted at rest using industry standard algorithms
- All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
- Encryption keys are managed securely with restricted access

---

## 8. Network and Infrastructure Security

- Firewalls and network segmentation protect production systems
- Vulnerability scanning is conducted regularly
- Penetration testing is conducted at least annually
- Security patches are applied according to a defined patch management schedule

---

## 9. Incident Response

- All employees must report suspected security incidents immediately to the Information Security Lead
- Incidents are managed according to the Incident Response Plan
- Post-incident reviews are conducted to identify root cause and prevent recurrence

---

## 10. Vendor and Third Party Management

- All vendors with access to company or customer data undergo a risk assessment before onboarding
- Data Processing Agreements are required for all vendors processing personal data
- Vendor security posture is reviewed annually

---

## 11. Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

- Backup procedures are documented and tested regularly
- A disaster recovery plan is maintained and tested annually
- Recovery time and recovery point objectives are defined for critical systems

---

## 12. Employee Security Awareness

- All employees complete security awareness training upon hire and annually thereafter
- Phishing simulations are conducted periodically
- Employees sign an acceptable use policy upon hire

---

## 13. Change Management

- All changes to production systems follow a documented change management process
- Changes are reviewed and approved before deployment
- Emergency changes are documented retroactively with appropriate justification

---

## 14. Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment or contract.

---

## 15. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed at least annually or following any significant change to the organisation's risk environment.
